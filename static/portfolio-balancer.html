WHY REBALANCING WORKS
01
SYSTEMATIC PROFIT-TAKING
When an asset class rises, it takes up more than its 20% share. Rebalancing forces you to sell at elevated prices and rotate into underperformers — mechanically implementing "sell high, buy low" without predicting the market. Try Crypto Bull Run, then rebalance, then simulate a crash.
02
UNCORRELATED ASSET CLASSES
Crypto, stocks, property, metals and bonds rarely crash simultaneously. A 70% crash in one class only destroys 14% of a balanced portfolio — versus 70% of a concentrated one. Try Stock Market Crash to see bonds and gold cushion the blow while equities collapse.
03
COMPOUNDING THE REBALANCE
The real power comes from repeating this across many cycles. Apply Crypto Bull Run → Rebalance → simulate a crash. The balanced portfolio locked in gains at the top. The concentrated investor rides it all the way back. Over decades, this discipline significantly outperforms emotional concentration.
